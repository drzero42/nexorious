Xbox Logo Attribution Requirements
==================================

Required Attribution:
Xbox and the Xbox logos are trademarks of Microsoft Corporation.

Legal Requirements:
- Must follow Microsoft trademark and brand guidelines
- Cannot alter Xbox logos in any manner (size, proportions, colors, elements, etc.)
- Cannot animate, morph, or distort perspective or appearance
- Must link back to https://www.xbox.com/ when used
- Only for indicating non-commercial fan sites related to Xbox

Non-Commercial Fansite Guidelines:
- May only display Xbox logos to indicate hosting non-commercial Xbox fan site
- Logos must link back to Xbox.com or Microsoft-hosted game sites
- Strict restrictions on modifications and usage

Commercial Usage:
- Developer resources require authorization
- Official press kit materials require media credentials or partner status
- Contact Microsoft for commercial licensing
