Telltale Games Storefront Logo
==============================

Source: Recreated from the official Telltale site icon (the three-dot mark).
URL: https://telltale.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/tt_site_icon.png
Downloaded: 2026-06-24

Description: Telltale's "three dots" brand mark — two circles above a single
circle, in a triangular arrangement. The original site icon is only a 16x16
PNG, so the SVGs here are a clean geometric recreation of that mark (not a
traced copy of the bitmap).

Files:
- telltale-icon-light.svg: Light variant (black circles) for light backgrounds
- telltale-icon-dark.svg:  Dark variant (white circles) for dark backgrounds

Both variants keep the brand-red bottom circle (#cc0000); only the two upper
circles flip between black and white per theme. This matches how other
brand-coloured storefront icons (e.g. playstation-store) retain their accent
colour across themes.
